What makes ICanLocalize better?

All translation work is done using our website translation, which eliminates all technical work in the way to translate entire websites.

Work less, get paid more

All you need to do is translate - nothing more. The system takes care of all the rest for you:

  • Get projects from clients - project will arrive at your PC directly.
  • Text extraction and website creation.
  • Send updates back to clients - your work is automatically sent to the client. Your delivery is documented.

As a result, you can concentrate just on translation and make more money in less time. We charge a 20% commission, which covers the translation software, our escrow service, getting you projects and all the support you'll ever need.

There's no sign-up fees, "featured listing" fees and other modern inventions. We get paid only when you get paid.

No work queue

We don't assign work to translators and don't have any favourites. You can get notified automatically as new projects become available and bid on as many projects as you like.

Clients choose their translators themselves, so if you can prove yourself, you'll get as much work as you like.

Safe payment environment

Clients deposit the full payment for the work when they choose translators. We hold this payment in escrow until the work completes.
