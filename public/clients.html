Self Managed Website Translation

ICanLocalize is a website translation agency that lets website owners and translators work directly.

How does it work?

  • Create your translation project and make it available to translators.
  • Receive bids from qualified translators.
  • Select the translator you want to work for you.
  • Deposit the payment in our system.
  • When you're 100% happy with the translation work, the translator gets paid.

Lowest cost for translation work

We give both you and the translator free translation software, made especially for handling websites.

You create your translation project on your PC. Translators use it to translate your website in context.

When the translator is done, you get the newly translated version of your website in ready to use condition. There's no manual work required - it's all fully automated.
